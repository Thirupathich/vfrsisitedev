New Webinar to Focus on Patient Concerns

Date : 21st Apr 2020

Authors :Vattikuti Foundstion

Focus on Patients: Let's Talk About Robotic Surgery signals the start of a new VF initiative.

Here is a description of the first program:

Robotic Surgery. You may have heard about it, but don’t really know what it's all about. Dr. Santosh Waigankar, a former Vattikuti Fellow now practicing at Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, has a fresh perspective as a young, highly- trained robotic surgeon. “Let’s talk about Robotic Surgery” is the topic of his Free Webinar on Saturday, October 17th, 6:30 PM IST, 9AM EDT. The Vattikuti Foundation has a history of supporting robotic surgery for close to 20-years. The popular Vattikuti Foundation Webinar Series has been a platform for Indian Surgeons to share content among themselves. Now, we are expanding to include new webinar programs especially for the public! If you have questions- we are here to provide answers! You will find honest answers from the new Vattikuti Foundation Focus on Patients Webinar Series. The public are invited to join in this program.

Please use this link: https://us02web.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_xY5Rs61DSnibjhDfT3mThQ

By : Dave Meinhard

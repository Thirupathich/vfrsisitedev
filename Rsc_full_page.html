Robotic Surgeons Council of India Video Library

Currently, there are nearly 200 videos available of presentations and videos from past Robotic Surgeons Council of India meetings. Here is a link to the website Media Library section:

https://vfrsi.vattikutifoundation.com/video/index

Copy the above link. paste it in your browser, open the window, then select "Robotic Surgeons Council of India" from the EVENTS dropdown- it's that simple!

Have you wanted to revisit an exceptionally interesting KS Robotic Surgery Award-winning video? Would you like to hear an excellent lecture from an International Faculty member of a past Robotic Surgeons Council of India (RSC) meeting? What about reviewing that special technique your colleague from _____ Hospital shared last year? The good new is- you can!

The Vattikuti Foundation website Media Library has nearly 200 videos available of presentations and videos from past Robotic Surgeons Council of India meetings. It is a free resource made available for RSC members- and anyone else searching for high quality robotic surgery information. Here is a link to the website Media Library section:

https://vfrsi.vattikutifoundation.com/video/index

Copy the above link, paste it in your browser, open the window, then select "Robotic Surgeons Council of India" from the EVENTS dropdown- it's that simple!

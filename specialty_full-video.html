Robotic Prostatectomy in High Risk and Locally Advanced Prostate Cancer

International Faculty for the Robotic Surgeons Council of India, Mr. Nikhil Vasdev, MBBS, MCh (Urol, FRCS (Urol), Consultant Urological and Robotic Surgeon Hertfordshire& South Bedfordshire Urological Cancer Centre, Department of Urology, Lister Hospital, UK; Assoc. Lecturer in Urology- University of Cambridge; Senior Clinical Lecturer in Urology- University of Hertfordshire, talks about how the robotic prostatectomy can be done in a "Very optimistic manner," in these high risk prostate cancer patients. 
He shares insight from Dr. Mani Menon and other studies, along with his many insights about high risk, locally advanced prostate cancer, which he says "Is a different beast!"
He describes the Lister "Neurosafe Technique," Pathology, Extended LND and more.  Lecture, with PPT's, CT images, animation and robotic surgery video.  23:46

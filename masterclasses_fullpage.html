Master Classes

Structured Hands-on Skills Training Programme in Robotic Radical Prostatectomy

Date : 05th Mar 2018 to 09th Mar 2018
Institute, City : Medanta Vattikuti Institute of Robotic Surgery, Medanta - the Medicity, Gurugram
International Faculty :

Dr. Narmada P. Gupta, Dr. Rajiv Yadav, Dr. Manav Suryavanshi, Dr. Sangeeta Khanna, Dr. Anil Mandhani, Dr. Kulbir Ahlawat, Dr. Varun Mittal, Dr. Dheeraj Gautam, Nikhil Khattar.

In addition, Dr. Vipul Patel will be performing live surgery via a televised webcast link from the USA.

Vattikuti Technologies product specialists: , Dr. Kaarthik, Mr. Hemant Rathi, Ms. Sonam and Mr. Satish will supervise Dry Lab and Simulator training.

Additional faculty TBA.

Coordinator : Mr. Abishek Singhal
Contact for registration : abishek.singhal@medanta.org

Medanta, the Medicity will offer a week-long course to train Urologists in Robotic Radical Prostatectomy. Titled: Structured Hands-on Skills Training Programme in Robotic Radical Prostatectomy, the course features five days of intensive training on the da Vinci Surgical System. Training includes: docking, anesthesia, simulator work, dry and wet lab sessions, OR efficiency, and many more topics taught by Medanta, National and International faculty. The Course Chairman is Dr. Narmada P. Gupta, Medanta Chairman (Academic Research) Urology. Course Directors are Dr. Rajiv Yadav and Dr. Manav Suryavanshi. The course will be offered four times in 2018.

Dates are: March 5 - 9, 2018

Sessions are limited to six surgeons. MCh, DNB degrees in Urology or equivalent are required. Fees are: INR: 50,000, USD: 1000 (International delegates).

The Vattikuti Foundation is pleased to suport these courses and offers sincere thanks to Dr. Gupta and the Medanta staff for hosting these training opportunities.
